## Precursors of Rap

1) Signifying / The Dozens
	- Oral word game

	- African origin

Example: “Signifyin’ Monkey”
- Character in a lot of African Mythology - trickster
- This predates hip hop music
- But it's like a rhythmic and articulation style

2) Jamaican Toasting
- yard dances / Sound system men/DeeJay
	- a way to hear American music
	- Play American records
- SSM become 1st Jamaican recording producers
- Producing for Yard Dances
	- They would try to make unique records so that people could only go to their yard dance
- Same song on both sides, one without vocals - Toasting - A side
- SSM would vocally improvise over the “B” side.
	- Strip down the song
	- Make a very open song
	- People would start to use a mic and talking over the record
		- They would either flex or diss people
