# The Counter Culture
Major movement in 1960s for change
- Influence of The Beats (Jack Kerouac, Allan Ginsberg)
-  Questioning the status quo was rare - never question authority 
- Jazz Beat
	- A Jazz Beat (Bebop)
	- Very difficult to play
-  Beaten down
	- Constantly being hunted by authorities 
- Beatitude:
	- Train our minds to see things as they really are
	- Break away from the mindset of consumerism 
- Revived in the early 1960s  
- Move away from ideology of parents
	- Cared more about experience rather than material things
		- Striving to see the world as it really as rather than material things
	- Hippies - rejection of mainstream conventional values
