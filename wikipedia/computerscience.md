- Programming Language Theory - different wants to describe computational processes
- Operating Systems
- Networks

Defns:

- Computer Architecture is the construction of computer components and computer-operated equipment
- AI and ML aim to synthesize goal-orientated processes such as problem-solving, decision-making, environmental adaptation, planning and learning found in humans and animals
- Natural Language Processing - understanding and processing textual and linguistic data

## History

- Wilhelm Schickard - created the first mechanical calculator in 1723
- Gottfried Leibniz made the first digital calc called the Stepped Reckoner
- Charles Babbage - made the first automatic mechanical calc called the Difference Engine > Which later became the Analytical Engine
- Jacquard Loom - created the punched card system
- 1865, Herman Hollerith created the tabulator - which uses punch cards to process statistical information
